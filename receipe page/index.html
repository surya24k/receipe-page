Grandma's Secret Apple Pie

This apple pie recipe has been in my family for generations and never fails to delight. The secret is in the perfect balance of sweet and tart flavors.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 6 Medium-sized apples,peeled and sliced

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the sugar and cinnamon.
  3. Toss the apples in the sugar-cinnamon mixture.
